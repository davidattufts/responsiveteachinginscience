How Do Batteries Work?

Transcripts

On Day 5, Kim's students had a passionate discussion about batteries and why there were positive and negative terminals. In the process they were spontaneously engaged in real scientific practice: reasoning, arguing, and bringing evidence to bear as they tried to understand the battery. These videos provide rich examples of these practices. While watching the first video, look for students' ideas and reasoning.
