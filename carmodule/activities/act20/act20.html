Teachers often combine several different types of "Exploring/Discussing Toy Cars"-type activities, including activities that involve regular toy cars (e.g., Ramps or Rubber Bands), and activities (such as Propeller Cars or Battery Cars) that involve toy cars with an embedded mechanism for motion.

Typically, students gather in small groups and explore one external method for moving regular toy cars, or one type of mechanistic car. Occasionally they explore more than one method, or more than one type of mechanistic car. Class discussion combines the discussions of different types of methods for moving regular cars and different types of mechanistic toy cars.
