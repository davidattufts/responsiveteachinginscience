Summary

The launching activity for the toy car module focused on having students suggest various ways for getting a toy car to move, and students spent four days working on it. Sharon introduced the activity by asking students to think individually, then discuss with a partner, about different ways they could get a toy car to move. The whole class then shared their ideas. The class then broke up into small groups to work at tables and draw their ideas on large presentation boards. After some time Sharon reassembled the class and called on members of different groups to bring up their presentation boards and discuss their ideas with the rest of the class. During this time Sharon and members of the class asked questions of the speaker. Some questions were intended to clarify what the speaker was trying to say, while other questions were about extending the ideas. Afterwards, students wrote down their ideas in their science journals.
